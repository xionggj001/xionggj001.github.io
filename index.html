About me

I am an incoming postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Computer Science at Harvard University, hosted by Prof. Milind Tambe, and supported by the Harvard Center for Research on Computation and Society (CRCS). I am currently a final-year PhD student in the Department of Applied Mathematics and the Department of Computer Science at Stony Brook University, supervised by Dr. Jian Li. Prior to my PhD, I received my B.E. from Sun Yat-sen University , Guangzhou, China, and my M.S. from the University of Kansas , Lawrence, both in Electrical Engineering.

Research Interest

My research interests include Reinforcement Learning, Multi-Armed Bandits, Federated/Decentralized Learning, Stochastic Optimization and Control.
